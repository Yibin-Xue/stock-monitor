# Brainstorming Skill

This skill helps generate creative ideas and solutions for various problems. It can be used for:

- Generating new product ideas
- Solving complex problems
- Creating marketing strategies
- Developing content ideas
- Exploring new approaches to challenges

When invoked, it will ask clarifying questions to understand the context and then provide a list of creative ideas and solutions.

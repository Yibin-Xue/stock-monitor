# Writing Plans Skill

This skill helps create structured writing plans and outlines for various types of writing projects. It can be used for:

- Academic papers and essays
- Blog posts and articles
- Books and book chapters
- Reports and white papers
- Presentations and speeches

When invoked, it will help organize thoughts, create a logical structure, and develop a comprehensive outline for the writing project.
